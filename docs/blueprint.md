# **App Name**: HealthScribe AI

## Core Features:

- User Profile: Securely collect and store user information including name, age, gender, location, health conditions, medications, allergies, and lifestyle factors. All health data is kept private and not shared.
- Symptom Intake: Interactive form to capture detailed information about symptoms including onset, frequency, severity, triggers, related symptoms, and medical history.
- AI Symptom Analysis: Employ a generative AI tool to analyze symptoms based on provided information and suggest potential explanations and safe OTC options.
- Immediate Relief Guidance: Suggest safe, non-invasive actions the user can take immediately to reduce discomfort.
- OTC Suggestion Engine: Recommends widely available over-the-counter medications (with generic names) and non-drug remedies to help with symptom relief, including contraindications.
- Self-Care Tips: Generate practical daily habits and lifestyle adjustments to support recovery and manage symptoms. The AI will reason about the specific needs of the user as identified during the onboarding flow.
- Medical Care Alert: Clearly indicate red-flag symptoms that necessitate prompt medical attention, differentiating between seeing a doctor soon versus seeking emergency care.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft blue (#64B5F6) to evoke a sense of calm and trust, without feeling overly sterile. This color promotes a sense of well-being.
- Background color: Very light blue (#E3F2FD) to complement the primary color and create a clean and approachable interface.
- Accent color: Light orange (#FFCC80) used sparingly to highlight important information or calls to action, creating contrast.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif, will provide a modern yet slightly warm feel, ensuring readability and approachability.
- Use clean, simple line icons to represent symptoms and conditions. Icons should be easily understandable and visually consistent.
- Design a clean, intuitive layout with ample whitespace to avoid overwhelming users. Use clear sections and headings for easy navigation.
- Incorporate subtle transitions and animations to guide users through the symptom analysis process and provide feedback, enhancing usability.
